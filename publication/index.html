OpenPeppol eDEC Specifications

The specifications listed here are mandatory in the Peppol eDelivery network.
Documents with a "Valid from" date in the future are not valid before that particular date.
Documents with a "Valid to" date in the past are no longer valid.
Documents without a "Valid to" date are valid until further notice.
